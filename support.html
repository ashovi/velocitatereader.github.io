Frequently Asked Questions

How do I import books?

Tap the + icon in your Library. You can import local PDF and TXT files directly from your device, or use the "Paste Text" option to quickly read articles from your clipboard.

Why does the app pause automatically?

Velocitate uses Smart Chapter Stops. The engine identifies major headings and pauses the RSVP stream to give your brain a moment of reflection before the next section. Just tap the screen to resume.

What is "Speed Ramping"?

Speed Ramping allows you to start reading at a comfortable pace (e.g., 300 WPM) and gradually accelerates to your goal speed over several seconds. This mimics a natural mental warm-up and reduces cognitive strain.

Is there a Dark Mode?

Yes. Velocitate is designed with high-contrast, low-light reading in mind. You can adjust the Universal Dimmer in the reader settings to fine-tune the brightness of both the text and the focus guides.
