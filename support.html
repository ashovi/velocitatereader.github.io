Common Questions

How do I import my own books? Tap the + icon in the Library. You can import local PDFs/TXT files, paste text from your clipboard, or download directly from a URL.
What is "Speed Ramping"? It gradually increases your reading speed from a comfortable starting WPM to your goal WPM over a set duration. This helps your brain "warm up" to higher speeds.
The app keeps pausing on "Chapter." Why? Velocitate includes Smart Stops. It automatically pauses at the start of new chapters to give you a moment to reflect before continuing. Just tap Play to resume.
