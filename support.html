Common Questions

How do I import my own books?

Tap the + icon in the Library. You can import local PDFs/TXT files, paste text from your clipboard, or download directly from a URL.

What is "Speed Ramping"?

It gradually increases your reading speed from a comfortable starting WPM to your goal WPM over a set duration. This helps your brain "warm up" to higher speeds and reduces fatigue.

The app pauses on "Chapter." Why?

Velocitate includes Smart Stops. It automatically pauses at the start of new chapters to give you a moment of reflection. Just tap the screen to resume.

Is there a Dark Mode?

Yes. Velocitate is designed with high-contrast, low-light reading in mind. You can adjust the Universal Dimmer in the reader settings to fine-tune the brightness of both the text and the focus guides.
