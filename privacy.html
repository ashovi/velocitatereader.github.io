Privacy Policy

Your privacy is fundamental to the design of Velocitate Reader. This policy outlines how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Velocitate Reader operates entirely on your device and does not require a user account or login.

2. Your Content

All books, documents, and text imported into Velocitate (via PDF, TXT, URL, or Clipboard) are stored locally in your device's secure sandbox. We do not have access to your library or the content you read.

3. Local Settings

Your reading preferences (WPM speed, font sizes, theme colors, and reading progress) are stored locally on your device using standard system storage. This data never leaves your device and is not shared with any third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Velocitate Reader does not use third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising frameworks. When you download a book from a URL, that request is made directly from your device to the host server, similar to a standard web browser.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this policy, you can reach us at:
support@velocitatereader.com
